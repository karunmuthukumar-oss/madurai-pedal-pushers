About the Community

Madurai Pedal Pushers

We are a passionate group of amateur and professional cyclists based in Madurai, India. Our goal is to promote cycling as a sport, lifestyle, and community engagement. By connecting Strava API logs to our group telegram bot, we track and display standings, climb ratios, and logs to challenge our limits week over week.
